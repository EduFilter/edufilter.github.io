How to Block or Remove YouTube on Your Computer: A Complete Guide

You’re already using EduFilter to find educational YouTube videos without distractions. This guide helps you take it a step further by completely blocking access to the regular YouTube website on your computer.

Here are two effective methods to block or restrict YouTube access:

1. Browser Extensions for Website Blocking

The easiest and most flexible method to block YouTube is by using browser extensions specifically designed to block websites.

  • BlockSite – Free Block Websites
  • BlockSite – Block Websites & Stay Focused
  • Block Site

Tip: Combining multiple blocking extensions can increase effectiveness.

2. Parental Controls and Family Safety Tools

If you want to control YouTube access for children or family members, parental control software is the most comprehensive approach. These tools block YouTube across devices, monitor usage, and allow you to set time limits.

  • Microsoft Family Safety
  • Google Family Link
  • Apple Screen Time and Parental Controls
