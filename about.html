EduFilter - About

This site offers an AI-powered educational YouTube filter that helps you stay focused by limiting video searches to educational content only.

Please note: filtering is AI-based and works primarily from video titles. While designed to be helpful, it’s not perfect — some non-educational videos may slip through, and some educational videos may be missed.

If you find any bugs, please report them via this Google form. It takes less than a minute to fill out.
