About This Tool

This site provides an AI-powered educational YouTube filter that helps users stay focused by limiting video searches to educational content only. The tool is designed to reduce distractions and promote a more productive Youtube experience.

Please note that the filtering is powered by artificial intelligence, and while it is designed to be helpful, it is not perfect. Some content may be incorrectly allowed or blocked.
